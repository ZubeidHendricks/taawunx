Our Vision

At TAAWUNX, our mission is to revolutionize SME collaboration in emerging markets through a Sharia-compliant, blockchain-based platform. We are committed to creating a transparent, secure, and ethical ecosystem that drives economic growth while adhering to Islamic principles.

Platform Features

  • Blockchain Transparency: A blockchain-based infrastructure ensures trust and clarity in all transactions and collaborations.
  • Sharia-Compliant Smart Contracts: Automate project management, payments, and dispute resolution in adherence to Islamic finance principles.
  • AI-Powered Matching: Connect with complementary SMEs for optimal project outcomes.
  • Decentralized Reputation System: Build trust through a transparent, peer-reviewed reputation system.
  • Ethical Profit Sharing: Tokenized profit sharing aligns with Islamic finance principles.
  • Ethical Project Filtering: Only halaal projects and businesses are featured.
  • Zakat Integration: Assist in calculating and distributing zakat on profits.
